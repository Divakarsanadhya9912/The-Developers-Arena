# Healthcare Data Analysis Insights

## Age Distribution
Most patients fall within the middle age group, indicating higher healthcare data representation in this segment.

## Stroke Distribution
Stroke cases are significantly lower compared to non-stroke cases, showing imbalance in the dataset.

## Age vs BMI
BMI tends to increase with age in many patients, suggesting potential risk factors.

## Glucose Levels
Glucose levels vary significantly across patients, indicating possible metabolic health issues.

## Key Observations
• Age and BMI may influence health risk factors.
• High glucose levels appear in some patient groups.
• Stroke cases are comparatively rare but important for prediction models.

# Weather Data Analysis Insights

## Temperature Trend
The temperature trend chart shows fluctuations throughout the year, reflecting seasonal changes.

## Rainfall Distribution
Rainfall values vary widely, with most days experiencing low rainfall and a few extreme rainy days.

## Monthly Temperature
Average temperatures are higher during summer months and lower during winter months, showing clear seasonal patterns.

## Extreme Weather Events
Extreme weather events are rare but occur when maximum temperature exceeds normal thresholds or rainfall is unusually high.

## Key Observations
• Seasonal weather patterns strongly influence temperature.
• Rainfall distribution is skewed toward low rainfall days.
• Extreme weather events occur occasionally but are significant.

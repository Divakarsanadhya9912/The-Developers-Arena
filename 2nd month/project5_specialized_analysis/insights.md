# Real Estate Price Analysis Insights

## Price Distribution
House prices vary widely depending on area, location, and property type.

## Area vs Price
Larger properties generally tend to have higher prices, showing a positive relationship between area and price.

## Property Type Analysis
Different property types such as apartments, houses, and villas have different average prices.

## Location Analysis
Properties located in city centers tend to be more expensive compared to rural or suburban locations.

## Key Observations
• Area strongly influences property price.
• Villas and houses tend to be more expensive than apartments.
• Location significantly impacts property valuation.

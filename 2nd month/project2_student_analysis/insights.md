# Student Performance Analysis – Key Insights

## 1. Pass / Fail Analysis
Out of 500 students:

- 391 students passed (78.2%)
- 109 students failed (21.8%)

The overall pass rate is strong, but nearly 22% of students are failing, which is significant.

Recommendation:
Introduce targeted academic support for students near the failing threshold.

---

## 2. Subject Performance

Average Scores:

- Math: 67.69 (Highest)
- English: 66.76
- Science: 65.74 (Lowest)

Math is the strongest performing subject.
Science shows comparatively lower performance and may require instructional improvements.

---

## 3. Attendance Impact on Performance

Correlation between attendance percentage and average score:
0.03

This indicates a very weak relationship between attendance and academic performance in this dataset.

Conclusion:
Attendance alone does not strongly determine academic success in this case.
Other factors may influence performance.

---

## 4. Overall Conclusion

- Majority of students are passing.
- Subject performance is relatively balanced.
- Attendance does not significantly impact performance in this dataset.

Further analysis could include:
- Gender-based performance comparison
- Scholarship vs Regular student performance
- Identifying top 10% performers
